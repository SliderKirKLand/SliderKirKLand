Project 1

A bunch of fun brushes to draw with. I was aiming to make the UI for this paint program
to somewhat what the early 90s asthetic. There are buttons, color picker, brushe size for
the user to mess around.

The brushes are fairly simple in nature, nothing too fancy or crazy. There are shapes brushes, normal brushes, and one wacky one.

I didn't meet that much trouble setting up the brushes, but the UI was pretty time consuming. It took me quite sometime to finalize everything to what I wanted. More info about the shortcuts are in the project.
