Project 3

"Tonight, your ass will be visited by three ghosts: my foot, my other foot, and a ghost!" -- Soldier from TF2
Excercise 5

For this project, our collective inspiration was drawn from nature's diverse tapestry. My contribution focused on crafting a digital duck within a numerical array and encapsulating the essence of a leaf within a text array. This assignment involved a series of mappings that brought these arrays to life in a multi-page digital sketchbook.

Alongside my creations, I incorporated arrays from Kyra, Alisha, and Daria, each bringing a unique perspective of nature to the digital canvas. These can be viewed by navigating through five interactive pages using the number keys 1-4, with any other key returning to the home page.
