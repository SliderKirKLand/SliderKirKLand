Project 4

This project reimagines the iPod Nano® as a mix of fun and responsibility. It blends the nostalgia of listening to music with the practicality of filing taxes, turning a playful device into something unexpectedly productive.

By combining escapism and obligation in one device, it makes us think about how technology shapes our daily lives. Now, you can enjoy your favorite playlists while tackling real-world responsibilities. This work invites you to reflect on how we juggle work and play in today’s fast-paced, multitasking world. It’s a reminder of how we balance fun and functionality in modern life.
